WELCOME TO HISTORICAL PAGE OF INDIA

LAL KILLA

Lal Kila also known as the Red Fort, is a historic fortress in Delhi.Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in 1648, it served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years. The fort is made of red sandstone.

PURI

The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha, is one of the most sacred Hindu temples and a key Char Dham pilgrimage site. It is dedicated to Lord Jagannath along with his siblings Balabhadra and Subhadra.

TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is a world-famous mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.Completed in 1648,it is a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.

KONARK

Konark is a historic town in Odisha, India, famous for the Konark Sun Temple.The Sun Temple was built in the 13th century by King Narasimha Deva I of the Eastern Ganga Dynasty.

QUTAB MINAR

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, India and the tallest brick minaret in the world at 73 meters.Built in 1193 by Qutb-ud-din Aibak, the founder of the Delhi Sultanate.

GOLDEN TEMPLE

The Golden Temple is the holiest shrine of Sikhism, located in Amritsar, Punjab.Built in 1604 by Guru Arjan Dev Ji, the fifth Sikh Guru.Surrounded by the Amrit Sarovar, believed to have healing properties.

KAPILAS

The Kapilash Temple, also known as the Mahadev Chandrashekhar Temple, is a famous Shiva temple located in Dhenkanal, Odisha.Situated at Kapilash Hill, about 2,239 feet above sea level.
